FAQs

Can I share and re-use Feedback Animal Cards?

Yes. This activity is free to use by anyone, for any purpose, as much as you like in its current form.

However, if you would like to:

  • Rebrand the Feedback Animal Cards activity
  • Label it as your own
  • Bundle it under a different name or identity
  • Build on it or adapt it in any way

Please contact us first to arrange a licensing agreement.
We’re open to collaboration, but we ask that credit and original branding are respected.

What personal information is being collected by the Feedback Animals website?

In short, none. We don’t collect any information that can identify individuals. You may have noticed that we don’t ask for your name, log-in or email address. Your results disappear when you refresh your screen - remember to take a screen shot if you want to save them.

We keep a basic activity log that includes things like which cards were selected or moved, along with a session ID and timestamp. We don’t store your IP address. We do store your country, region and city.

These logs help us understand how people are using the tool so we can make it better — that’s it.
